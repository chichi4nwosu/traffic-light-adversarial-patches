# Research Questions

## Main Research Direction

How can adversarial patches fool traffic light detection systems?
Can we intentionally fool the traffic light detector?

## Questions

1. How does the patch work?
2. What detector did they use?
3. What dataset did they use?
4. How was attack success measured?
5. Can the attack be improved?
6. Can the attack be detected?
7. Can the attack transfer to different models?
