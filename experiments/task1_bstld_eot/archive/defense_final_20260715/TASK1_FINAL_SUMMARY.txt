MAIN CONCLUSION
---------------
Patch-augmented adversarial training substantially improved robustness
to the targeted Red-to-Green EOT patch attack.

The original fixed patch TMR decreased from 77.02% to 0.00% against
the defended detector.

An adaptive white-box attacker partially recovered attack effectiveness,
achieving 28.26% TMR against the defended detector. This remained
substantially below the 77.02% TMR observed against the original model.

The defense therefore improves robustness but does not provide complete
robustness against an adaptive adversary.
